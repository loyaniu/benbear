Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a transaction?

Tap the "+" button in the tab bar. You can either use voice input to describe your transaction naturally, or switch to manual mode to enter details directly.

How does voice input work?

Simply tap the microphone button and speak naturally. For example, say "Spent 25 dollars on lunch at Shake Shack" and we'll automatically extract the amount, category, and note.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! BenBear works offline. Your transactions are saved locally and automatically sync when you're back online.

How do I edit or delete a transaction?

Go to the Transactions tab, find the transaction you want to modify, and swipe left to reveal the delete option, or tap on it to edit.

How do I add custom categories?

Go to Settings > Categories. You can add new categories with custom names, icons, and colors for both expenses and income.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely using Google Firebase. Only you can access your financial data.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
